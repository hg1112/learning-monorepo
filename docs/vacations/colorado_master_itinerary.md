# Colorado Master Itinerary — June 4–9, 2026

## Day 1 (June 4): Rocky Mountain National Park, Grand Lake & Hot Springs
- **4:00 AM** — Land in Denver, pick up rental car
- **5:30 AM – 7:00 AM** — Drive north to Rocky Mountain National Park (~1.5 hrs)
- **7:00 AM – 9:00 AM** — Bear Lake alpine glow + drive Trail Ridge Road west through the park
- **9:30 AM – 10:30 AM** — Grand Lake: scenic mountain town at the west entrance of RMNP
- **11:00 AM – 12:30 PM** — Hot Sulphur Springs (~30 min from Grand Lake): soak and unwind
- **12:30 PM – 3:30 PM** — Drive south to Carbondale via US-40 → I-70 (~3 hrs)
- **4:00 PM** — Check in to Carbondale Airbnb

**Overnight: Carbondale (Airbnb) — Check-in**

- **8:30 AM – 9:00 AM** — Drive to Glenwood Springs (~25 min)

- **1:30 PM – 4:00 PM** — ATV Off-Roading Tour (e.g. Glenwood Adventure Company)
- **4:30 PM – 5:00 PM** — Drive back to Carbondale (~25 min)

## Day 3 (June 6): Maroon Bells, Gondolas, and Snow-Capped Passes
- **7:00 AM – 7:35 AM** — Drive to Aspen shuttle pickup (~35 min)
- **7:30 AM – 10:30 AM** — Early morning shuttle to Maroon Bells (iconic peaks mirrored in lake)
- **11:00 AM – 1:00 PM** — Silver Queen Gondola up Ajax Mountain; lunch at Sundeck (11,212 ft)
- **1:00 PM – 4:00 PM** — Drive east over Independence Pass (CO-82): continental divide summit (12,095 ft), Twin Lakes photos
- **4:00 PM – 6:00 PM** — Continue to Frisco / Lake Dillon area

**Overnight: Carbondale (Airbnb) — Check-out next morning**

## Day 4 (June 7): Alpine Kayaking, Steam Train & Caves
- **8:00 AM** — Check out, drive east to Frisco (~1 hr 15 min)
- **9:00 AM – 11:30 AM** — Kayaking on Lake Dillon at Frisco Bay Marina or Dillon Marina
- **11:30 AM – 12:15 PM** — Drive east through Eisenhower Tunnel to Georgetown (~30 min)
- **12:30 PM – 2:30 PM** — Georgetown Loop Railroad: historic steam train over Devil's Gate High Bridge
- **2:30 PM – 4:30 PM** — Drive south to Manitou Springs (~1.5 hrs)
- **5:00 PM – 7:00 PM** — Cave of the Winds Mountain Park: cave tour + outdoor adventures (Terror Dactyl, Wind Walker course)

**Overnight: Colorado Springs (Airbnb) — Check-in**

- **Evening** — Relax in Colorado Springs
