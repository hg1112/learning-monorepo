# Moab vs Sedona — Honest Comparison

**Context:** First anniversary, first-time skydiving, no swimming, no alcohol, June 3–10 (flex to June 1–12)

---

## Activity-by-Activity

| Experience | Moab | Sedona |
|------------|------|--------|
| **Skydiving** | ★★★★★ — 16,500 ft over Canyonlands. Best skydiving backdrop in the US. | ★★★★ — Over red rock spires and canyon country. Excellent but less dramatic than Canyonlands. |
| **2nd adrenaline hit** | ★★★★★ — Dry canyoneering (rappel into slot canyons). Completely unique, nowhere else like it. | ★★★★★ — Tandem paragliding over Cathedral Rock. Different kind of thrill — flying vs. falling. |
| **Grand Canyon** | ✗ — 5.5 hrs away, not feasible as a day trip. | ★★★★★ — 2 hrs to South Rim. **Grand Canyon helicopter INTO the canyon** is the best thing you can add to any Southwest trip. |
| **Hot air balloon** | ★★★★ — Over canyon country | ★★★★★ — Over Sedona red rocks at sunrise. Considered one of the best balloon rides in the US. |
| **Jeep/off-road** | ★★★★★ — Hell's Revenge, Shafer Trail. World-class 4WD terrain. | ★★★★ — Pink Jeep Tours (Broken Arrow). Excellent, more guided/curated. |
| **Via ferrata** | ✗ — Not available in Moab proper | ★★★★ — Sedona has a dedicated via ferrata route (iron rungs on cliffs, harness + guide, no experience needed). Third adrenaline activity. |
| **National parks** | ★★★★★ — Arches + Canyonlands. 2,000+ arches, Mesa Arch, Dead Horse Point. | ★★★ — No NP in Sedona itself. Red Rock State Park is beautiful but not the same scale. |
| **Resort/stay** | ★★★★ — Under Canvas Moab (glamping, romantic but rustic) | ★★★★★ — Enchantment Resort (built INTO Boynton Canyon, infinity pool facing red cliffs, Mii amo spa). The most romantic resort stay in Arizona. |
| **Spa** | ★★★ — Limited spa options in Moab | ★★★★★ — Mii amo at Enchantment. One of the top destination spas in the US. |
| **Town vibe** | Adventure town — gear shops, casual, outdoorsy | More upscale — galleries, art, couples-friendly. Better for an anniversary feel. |
| **Drive from airport** | SLC → 3.5 hrs | PHX → 2 hrs. Easier. |

## The Tiebreaker: Grand Canyon Helicopter

This is what Sedona has that Moab cannot match.

Papillon Grand Canyon Helicopters operates **floor landings** — you fly INTO the Grand Canyon, 4,000 ft below the rim, and land on the canyon floor next to the Colorado River. You walk around on the canyon floor for ~20 minutes before flying back up. 

This is not a sightseeing flyover. You are standing at the bottom of one of the seven natural wonders of the world, looking UP at 4,000 ft of layered canyon walls. It is a completely different experience from standing on the rim.

- Cost: ~$500–600/person for the floor landing tour
- Duration: ~4 hrs total (drive to South Rim from Sedona, tour, drive back)
- Book: papillon.com — fills weeks in advance in June
- No swimming. No hiking required. Just fly in, stand there, fly out.

## What Sedona Can Stack in 8 Days

1. **Hot air balloon** at sunrise over red rock country
2. **Skydiving** over Sedona
3. **Tandem paragliding** over Cathedral Rock / Bell Rock
4. **Grand Canyon helicopter** floor landing (day trip, 2 hrs each way)
5. **Via ferrata** — cliff face climbing with iron rungs, guided
6. **Private Pink Jeep Tour** — Broken Arrow backcountry
7. **Devil's Bridge hike** + Cathedral Rock at sunset
8. **Mii amo spa** couples treatment (anniversary afternoon)

That's 5 distinct adrenaline/adventure activities vs Moab's 3 (skydive, canyoneer, balloon). And Enchantment Resort is a significantly more romantic base than anything in Moab.

---

## What Moab Does Better

- **Skydiving view** — Canyonlands from 16,500 ft is objectively more spectacular than anything near Sedona
- **Slot canyon canyoneering** — there is nothing like this in Sedona. The experience of rappelling into a 2-ft wide slot canyon is completely unique.
- **Arches National Park** — 2,000 natural arches in one park. Nothing comparable near Sedona.
- **Dead Horse Point** — the best single overlook in the American Southwest
- **Scale** — Moab's canyon country is vast and alien. Sedona is beautiful but more contained.

## Honest Verdict

| If you want... | Pick |
|----------------|------|
| Best skydiving view in the US | **Moab** |
| Most adventure activities stacked | **Sedona** (5 vs 3) |
| Grand Canyon helicopter floor landing | **Sedona** (only option) |
| Most romantic resort stay | **Sedona** (Enchantment) |
| Most unique geological experience (slot canyons + Arches) | **Moab** |
| Easier logistics | **Sedona** (PHX, 2 hr drive) |

**For a first anniversary specifically:** Sedona edges Moab. The Grand Canyon helicopter is a once-in-a-lifetime experience that you can only add here. The Enchantment Resort is a meaningfully better anniversary stay. And you get more distinct adventure firsts (paragliding + via ferrata vs canyoneering).

**Moab is the better pure adventure trip.** Sedona is the better anniversary adventure trip.
