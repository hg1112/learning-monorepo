## Top Destinations — Rebuilt for No Swimming

### 1. Moab, Utah ⭐ Top Pick
**Fly into:** SLC → 3.5 hr drive

The slot canyons around Moab are completely dry — you rappel down ropes into canyon walls that are sometimes only 2 feet wide, carved smooth by ancient water into surreal shapes. Combine with skydiving and jeep tours and you have 3 genuinely different adrenaline experiences without going near water.

**Activities:**
- **Skydive Canyonlands** — 16,500 ft over Arches + Canyonlands. Best skydiving view in the US.
- **Dry canyoneering** — rappel into slot canyons (Desert Highlights, Moab Cliffs & Canyons). Zero water, zero swimming. Fully guided.
- **Private off-road jeep tour** — Hell's Revenge, Shafer Trail, private guide
- **Arches National Park** — Delicate Arch, Landscape Arch, fins + spires
- **Dead Horse Point** — 2,000 ft above the Colorado River bend, best overlook in Utah
- **Hot air balloon** — sunrise float over canyon country (optional, very romantic)

**Stay:** Under Canvas Moab (glamping — canvas tents, red rock views, fire pits, stars) or Hoodoo Moab  
**Budget:** ~$2,500–4,000

---

### 2. Las Vegas + Surroundings — Most Concentrated
**Fly into:** LAS (Frontier flies direct to Las Vegas)

Las Vegas is the ultimate adventure base — within 2.5 hrs you can reach 4 completely different landscapes. You never have to worry about food, logistics, or booking lead time. Everything just works.

**Activities from Vegas:**
- **Skydiving** — Skydive Las Vegas (Jean, NV, 30 min south) — desert + mountain views
- **Grand Canyon helicopter tour** — fly into the canyon, land on the floor, walk around. One of the most surreal experiences in the US. (~$400–600/person)
- **Grand Canyon Skywalk** — glass bridge extending over the canyon rim (Grand Canyon West, 2.5 hrs)
- **Zion National Park** — Angels Landing hike (chains-assisted cliff trail, completely dry, unreal views), Observation Point. 2.5 hrs from Vegas.
- **Red Rock Canyon** — guided rock climbing or scrambling, 30 min from Vegas
- **Valley of Fire State Park** — alien red rock formations, 1 hr from Vegas, best at sunrise
- **Fly LINQ Zipline** — zip over the Vegas strip at night (tourist but fun)

**Stay:** Stay on the Strip for 2–3 nights (easier logistics), then switch to a lodge near Zion or Grand Canyon for the outdoors nights  
**Budget:** ~$2,000–4,000 (Vegas hotel pricing varies wildly — book early)

**Why it works:** This is the only destination where a Grand Canyon helicopter flight is a realistic day trip. That experience — flying INTO the canyon — is bucket-list level and needs zero physical ability.

---

### 3. Sedona, Arizona — Most Romantic Adventure
**Fly into:** PHX → 2 hr drive (Frontier flies to Phoenix)

Sedona is the most visually beautiful adventure destination in the US that isn't a national park. Red rock towers, canyon light, pink sky at sunset. Every adventure activity here has a backdrop that makes it feel cinematic.

**Activities:**
- **Tandem paragliding** — Sedona Skyadventures: you and a certified pilot launch off a red rock ridge and fly over the canyon. No swimming, no effort — just flying. Completely different experience from skydiving (slower, longer, more scenic).
- **Skydiving** — Sedona Sky Ventures or fly from Cottonwood airport. Red rock + canyon views from above.
- **Pink Jeep Tour** — most famous adventure tour in Sedona. Private jeep climbs over slickrock formations, goes places a normal car never could. Broken Arrow trail has views that don't look real.
- **Via ferrata** — Sedona has a via ferrata route (iron rungs on cliff faces, harness + guide). No swimming.
- **Hot air balloon at sunrise** — most romantic thing you can do in Sedona. The red rocks at dawn from 1,000 ft above are extraordinary.
- **Devil's Bridge hike** — natural sandstone arch, 6 AM before heat, very doable

**Stay:** Enchantment Resort (built into Boynton Canyon, most romantic resort setting in Arizona)  
**Budget:** ~$2,500–4,500 (Enchantment is pricey but this is your anniversary)

**Why it's special for an anniversary:** Sedona is the only destination on this list where every single activity has a jaw-dropping romantic backdrop. The paragliding + hot air balloon combination is unlike anything you'd do anywhere else.

## Final Recommendation

**Moab** for the best skydiving in the US + dry canyoneering (rappelling into slot canyons needs no swimming, it's actually one of the coolest activities you can do). The Under Canvas Moab stay makes it a real anniversary trip, not just an adventure trip.

**Sedona** if you want to swap raw adrenaline for more romance. Tandem paragliding over red rock canyons + hot air balloon at sunrise + Enchantment Resort is a different kind of memorable — more cinematic, less intense.

**Las Vegas** if you want the Grand Canyon helicopter experience — it's the one thing no other destination offers and it's legitimately on most people's bucket lists.
